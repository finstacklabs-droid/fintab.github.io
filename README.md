FinTab Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 9, 2026

Welcome to FinTab (“we”, “our”, or “the app”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how FinTab handles your information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

FinTab is designed to help users manage personal finances, expenses, budgets, and credit card tracking.

Currently, FinTab primarily stores your financial data locally on your device.

Depending on features you use, the app may collect or process:

Expense and income entries entered by you
Budget and financial tracking information
Credit card and billing details entered by you
Reminder and notification preferences
App usage data required for app functionality
2. Data Storage

Your financial data is primarily stored locally on your device and is not sold to third parties.

If cloud backup, account sync, or online services are introduced in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

3. Permissions Used

FinTab may request certain permissions to provide app functionality:

Notifications — Used for bill reminders, budget alerts, recurring reminders, and due date notifications.
Storage Access (if applicable) — Used for backup, restore, export, or import features.

Permissions are only used for intended app functionality.

4. Data Sharing

FinTab does not sell your personal or financial data to third parties.

We do not share your financial information except:

When required by law
To protect app security or prevent abuse
If future cloud backup or sync services are explicitly enabled by the user
5. Third-Party Services

FinTab may use trusted third-party services or libraries for:

App functionality
Notifications
Crash reporting
Analytics (if enabled in future versions)

These services may process limited technical information necessary for app operation.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your information and improve app security. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy

FinTab is not intended for children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
